When you invest in FD, you can earn an interest rate on the amount deposited, which compounds over time and enables you to grow your savings.


Fixed deposits are a high-interest yielding term deposit and offered by banks in India. There is No Investment option in India that is as popular as Fixed Deposit (FD). This makes Fixed Deposit, one of the best ways to save money and also earn interest on the same.


A fixed deposit (FD) is a financial instrument provided by banks or NBFCs which provides investors a higher rate of interest than a regular savings account, until the given maturity date.

 

Benefits of Fixed Deposit

  • ⇒Fixed Returns
  • ⇒Flexible Investment Tenure
  • ⇒Tax benefits
  • ⇒Rewarding and Simple
  • ⇒Residents of India can open these accounts for a minimum of 7 days.
  • ⇒Investing in a fixed deposit earns you a higher interest rate than depositing your money in a savings account.

Advantages of Fixed Deposit

  • ⇒Low risk
  • ⇒Secured
  • ⇒Returns are assured
  • ⇒Safe investment
  • ⇒Higher rate of interest (Eg. 7% p.a)
  • ⇒Provide flexibility

Disadvantages of Fixed Deposit

  • ⇒No flexibility to access your funds
  • ⇒Locked-in funds
  • ⇒Penalties on withdrawal
  • ⇒Fixed interest rate
